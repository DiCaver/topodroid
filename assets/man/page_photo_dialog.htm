SHOT/SKETCH PHOTO

This dialog is opened from the Photo button in the secondary Shot edit dialog, or when a "photo" point or a photo xsection are inserted in the sketch window.

It has a text field for the comment to the photo you are going to take.

Uncheck the box TopoDroid camera if you want to use a Camera app instead of the TopoDroid camera.

Buttons:

  • OK closes the dialog and starts to take a photo.
  • Cancel closes the dialog.

TopoDroid camera
This is a simple implementation of a camera. Its unique feature is that it stores the azimuth and inclination of the device in exif tags in the image file. The azimuth is stored as GPS_LONGITUDE and the inclination as GPS_LATITUDE (with sign).
The following exif tags are set by TopoDroid:

  • Software: "TopoDroid" and its version, separated by a space
  • Orientation:
  • Datatime: timestamp
  • GPS latitude: inclination
  • GPS latitude ref: "N" or "S"
  • GPS longitude: azimuth
  • GPS longitude ref: "E"
  • Image description: azimuth and inclination in 1/100°, separaated by a space

For "photo" point or photo xsection the display of te TopoDroid camera has a centered overlaid cross-hair.

The TopoDroid camera has three buttons on the lower right.

  • camera. Red: ready, tap to take a picture. Blue: on hold, tap to turn camera active.
  • save saves the picture
  • back closes the window
With the screen up the picture is portrait. When the screen is turned to the left or to the rigth the picture is landscape.

Zoom buttns are brought up by tapping the screen in the mid-bottom. To go through large zoom steps tap the zoom buttons repeatedly fast.
